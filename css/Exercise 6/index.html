The Best Pasta Recepie

Ingredients:

  • lb spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Drain spaghetti and add to the skillet.
  5. Toss with parsley, salt, and pepper.
  6. Serve hot.
